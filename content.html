About us 

We are Camperette,  

Camperette is founded in February 2024. Safety while traveling for females is a issue and we want to take action to support the female travel community. We want female to feel safe and that is why we created Camperette. The service that we offer is insights and tips on traveling in a campervan. Buy our exclusive products that make your travel journey safer.
